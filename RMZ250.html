the RM-Z250, an all-new model Suzki came up with in order to meet the requirements of riders who favor a strong motocross competitor with looks similar to those of the RM250. The extra Z in the badge introduces a bulletproof chassis, strong Kayaba suspensions plus a host of competition-grade upgrades and tricks aimed at turning you from a mere competitor into podium pretender. This is one of the bikes developed as a part of the Suzuki-Kawasaki alliance. Suzuki engineered the engine, while Kawasaki did the "leg work", so this is why the RM-Z250 is such a spectacular performer.
